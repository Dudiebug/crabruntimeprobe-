# CrabSyncV2 Research Roadmap

These are RuntimeProbe research phases in recommended planning order. Each RuntimeProbe phase is read-only unless explicitly marked as a future CrabSyncV2-only sandbox. Stop after any crash, stale artifact warning, unexpected gate, or evidence contradicting the phase contract.

## P2P Baseline Track

| Phase | Goal | Required config gates | Allowed | Forbidden | Expected evidence file | Success criteria | Crash stop condition | CrabSyncV2 decision unlocked |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| `p2p-visibility-baseline` | Establish peer-visible category matrix across host/joined contexts. | Existing read-only visibility gates; all write/RPC gates false. | Capped read-only visibility checks for health/equipment/crystals/slots/inventory surfaces. | Writes, mutating RPCs, deep inventory traversal beyond proven layers. | `access_evidence_<session>.jsonl`. | Visibility matrix with explicit proven/unresolved markers. | Any crash or unsafe deep access marker. | Game-native P2P baseline scope. |
| `p2p-health-convergence-read` | Verify whether peer-visible health surfaces are sufficient for deterministic convergence planning. | `allowHealthProbes = true`; no unscoped `CrabHC`; writes/RPC false. | PlayerState-scoped health sampling across lifecycle contexts. | Unscoped `CrabHC`, writes, mutating RPCs. | `access_evidence_<session>.jsonl`. | Stable read snapshots by role/context with convergence inputs captured. | Crash or evidence of unsafe source path drift. | Health convergence model inputs. |
| `p2p-resource-convergence-read` | Verify deterministic convergence inputs for equipment/crystals/slots. | Resource/equipment read gates true; writes/RPC false. | Capped cross-player reads for proven resource/equipment paths. | Writes, mutating RPCs, custom payload injection. | `access_evidence_<session>.jsonl`. | Stable peer-visible snapshots and range-clamp validation inputs. | Crash, stale identity mismatch, or unsafe path regression. | Resource convergence planning. |
| `p2p-carrier-discovery-read` | Discover whether any safe replicated non-gameplay carrier candidate exists for `CrabSyncBlock`. | Dedicated carrier discovery gate; writes/RPC false. | Read-only candidate discovery with strict safety tagging. | Any writes, payload mutation, gameplay-field hijacking. | `access_evidence_<session>.jsonl`. | Candidate list with explicit safe/unsafe/unresolved classification. | Crash or evidence of gameplay-critical coupling. | Whether carrier module can remain in planning. |
| `p2p-carrier-visibility-watch` | Observe candidate carrier visibility across host/joined transitions. | Discovery-read success plus watch gate; writes/RPC false. | Read-only lifecycle visibility watch on candidate fields. | Writes, mutating RPCs, identity/save field mutation. | `access_evidence_<session>.jsonl`. | Candidate remains visible/stable without corruption signals. | Crash, stale state, or identity/save-risk signal. | Carrier viability confidence. |
| `p2p-carrier-capacity-read` | Infer observed capacity, type, cadence, and reset behavior from natural values only. | Candidate already classified safe to read; writes/RPC false. | Read-only observation of natural values and length/type/cadence metadata. | Synthetic payloads, writes, mutating RPCs, arbitrary object crawling. | `access_evidence_<session>.jsonl`. | Candidate capacity/cadence is documented or marked insufficient/unresolved. | Crash, privacy leak, or scope expansion beyond candidate path. | Whether a `CrabSyncBlock` strawman can fit. |
| `p2p-carrier-write-smoke` *(future, not RuntimeProbe default)* | Minimal controlled write smoke only after carrier read proof and explicit approval. | Future CrabSyncV2 sandbox gates only; RuntimeProbe default still read-only. | Manual tiny-scope smoke writes in isolated sandbox. | RuntimeProbe default mutation, broad writes, production usage. | Separate CrabSyncV2 sandbox evidence. | Write path behavior characterized with rollback/abort criteria. | Any crash/save corruption/UI desync signal. | Carrier write policy viability. |

Carrier template/status docs exist for future evidence outputs: [P2P Carrier Candidates](P2P_CARRIER_CANDIDATES.md), [P2P Carrier Visibility Matrix](P2P_CARRIER_VISIBILITY_MATRIX.md), [P2P Carrier Unsafe Paths](P2P_CARRIER_UNSAFE_PATHS.md), and [P2P Carrier Write-Smoke Plan](P2P_CARRIER_WRITE_SMOKE_PLAN.md). They do not prove a carrier exists and do not authorize write-smoke work.

[P2P Carrier Evidence Mapping](P2P_CARRIER_EVIDENCE_MAPPING.md) is a prerequisite for any future generated/status updates to those carrier docs; it defines classification rules only and does not implement collectors or import tooling.

[P2P Carrier Discovery Phase Contracts](P2P_CARRIER_DISCOVERY_PHASE_CONTRACTS.md) and [P2P Carrier Safety Gates](P2P_CARRIER_SAFETY_GATES.md) are prerequisites for future implementation discussions of carrier research phases. They define read-only boundaries and planning gate names only.

[P2P Carrier Readiness Checklist](P2P_CARRIER_READINESS_CHECKLIST.md) is required before any `CrabSyncBlock` design depends on a carrier. It does not prove a carrier exists or authorize write-smoke.

## Inventory Proof Track

| Phase | Goal | Required config gates | Allowed | Forbidden | Expected evidence file | Success criteria | Crash stop condition | CrabSyncV2 decision unlocked |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| `inventory-element-da-read` | Prove safe local item element + DA identity read path. | Deep inventory read gate(s) explicitly enabled; write/RPC false. | Capped wrapper/element/DA read sequence on proven parent. | Bulk traversal, writes, mutating RPCs. | `access_evidence_<session>.jsonl`. | First-item DA identity read succeeds or fails safely with clear markers. | Crash or invalid/stale object signal. | Item identity schema feasibility. |
| `inventoryinfo-scalar-read` | Prove safe local `InventoryInfo.Level` and `AccumulatedBuff` reads. | `allowInventoryInfoProbes = true`; write/RPC false. | Capped scalar reads on proven item path. | Enhancements traversal beyond phase scope, writes, RPCs. | `access_evidence_<session>.jsonl`. | Scalars captured or safe nil/error with clampability markers. | Crash or parent mismatch. | Metadata preservation feasibility. |
| `enhancements-read` | Prove safe `Enhancements` visibility (shape/count/value via staged subphases). | InventoryInfo proof complete; explicit enhancements gate; write/RPC false. | Staged capped enhancements reads per approved subphase. | Bulk traversal, writes, mutating RPCs. | `access_evidence_<session>.jsonl`. | Shape/count/value representation understood without instability. | Any crash or ambiguous value semantics. | Full item metadata model completeness. |
| `joined-client-replay-proven-phases` | Replay each proven local inventory phase as joined client. | Only matching proven phase gates; no new deeper gates. | Same read-only operations already proven locally. | New depth increases, writes, mutating RPCs. | `access_evidence_<session>.jsonl`. | Joined-client evidence matches local safety envelope. | Crash, stale session, or role instability. | Joined-client inventory reader eligibility. |
| `remote-inventory-visibility-research` | Determine remote inventory item identity/metadata visibility limits. | Minimal remote visibility gates under review; write/RPC false. | Capped remote PlayerState inventory visibility checks. | Writes, mutating RPCs, unsafe deep traversal. | `access_evidence_<session>.jsonl`. | Remote item identity/metadata classified as proven/unresolved/unavailable. | Crash or raw identity leak. | Whether inventory can be P2P-only or needs proven carrier. |
